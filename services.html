Services

Services are cruicial to aiding family members when one is in pallaitive care. Services help provide support, whilst keeping the family knowledgable about what to expect in the future.

Legal Services

The passing of a loved one unfortunately comes with its own legal procedures that can disrupt a family's healing process. Services are available to help the family both understand these procedures, aswell as take the hassle of dealing with these proceedings off their shoulders. Legal services can help:

  • Reading, understanding and carrying out what was outlined in the will
  • Assigning the power of attorney if a family member is too ill to do so themselves
  • Certifying the family member's passing with births deaths and marriages
  • Understand and plan for estate taxes
  • Provide legal assistance for disputes in the will

Funeral and burial arrangements

Services are available to understand what a family member wants to happen to their body once they pass on. They can also aid the family in planning a Funeral and burial/cremation service.

Emotional support

Services are available for both the family and the loved one to understand what is happening and prepare themselves emotionally for the passing. These services can help:

  • Provide services to the family at home whilst they grieve
  • Provide the family with grief counselling
  • Provide the family with religeous support
